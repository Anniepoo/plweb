Tar image of a relocatable SWI-Prolog installation. This merged image
was created on Ubuntu 10.10 (64-bit) and Debian 6.0 (32-bit). It
contains copies of shared libraries that are less widely available
and/or suffer regularly from version issues. If you are a one-time user,
check whether your Linux distro provides SWI-Prolog. If you are a
frequent user, consider to download and [[compile][</build/unix.html>]]
the source.

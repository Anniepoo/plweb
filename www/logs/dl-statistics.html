SWI-Prolog download statistics

Above is a graphical representation of the download statistics for SWI-Prolog from http://www.swi-prolog.org. Note that SWI-Prolog also appears on many Linux distributions and is shipped with various Prolog text-books.

Since March 2009, the website is served by Prolog itself. Unlike before, failed and cancelled downloads are now excluded from the counts.

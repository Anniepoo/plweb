---+ Publications about SWI-Prolog

Most of the time I managed to stick with writing code and documentation.
At rare weak moments I am pursuaded to write articles about aspects of
SWI-Prolog or its libraries. If you use SWI-Prolog and write academic
publiciations, please consider citing one of these papers.
